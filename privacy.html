Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

Short version: we are a wrap shop, not a data business. We keep the contact details you send us so we can quote your vehicle and reach you about it. We run advertising through Meta, which involves anonymized visit data from this website. We do not sell your information to anyone.

Who we are

This website is operated by ZAP WRAP LLC, a vinyl wrap studio based in Naples, Florida, serving Naples, Bonita Springs, Marco Island, Fort Myers and Cape Coral. In this policy "we", "us" and "the studio" mean ZAP WRAP LLC. "You" means anyone visiting zapwrapnaples.com or contacting the studio.

What we collect

1. Information you send us directly

When you message us on Instagram, Facebook or WhatsApp, call or text the studio, or fill in the consult form on this site, we receive whatever you choose to send. In practice that is usually:

  • your name or social media handle
  • your phone number, and your email address if you give one
  • your vehicle: year, make, model, condition
  • photos of the vehicle and reference images of finishes you like
  • anything else you write in the message

The consult form on this site does not run through a server of ours. Submitting it opens WhatsApp with the details you typed already filled in, so nothing is sent until you press send in WhatsApp yourself.

2. Advertising and analytics data from this website

This site loads the Meta Pixel (pixel ID 1584101513429246), a standard advertising tool from Meta Platforms used by most businesses that advertise on Instagram and Facebook. It records that a browser visited pages on this site and reports that back to Meta so our ads reach people who are actually looking for vehicle wraps, and so we can tell whether an ad worked.

Besides the page view, the pixel records two moments that tell us whether an ad worked: the consult form being submitted, and a WhatsApp link or the studio phone number being tapped. Only the fact that it happened is reported. None of the details you typed travel with it.

The pixel does not receive your name, your phone number or anything you typed into the form. What it does handle is technical and behavioral data: IP address, browser and device type, pages viewed, and Meta cookie identifiers. Meta may be able to link that to an existing Facebook or Instagram account. Meta acts as an independent controller of that data under its own terms, which you can read in the Meta Privacy Policy.

3. Technical data from hosting and fonts

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages, and typefaces are loaded from Google Fonts. Like any web host or font service, they receive the standard request data needed to serve the page, including your IP address and browser type. We do not run our own server logs, our own analytics, or any tracking beyond the Meta Pixel described above.

Why we use it, and for how long

Contact details and vehicle information are used for exactly two things: preparing your quote and communicating with you about the job. We keep enquiry and customer records for as long as needed to service the work and meet ordinary business and tax obligations in Florida, after which they are deleted. Advertising data is retained by Meta under Meta's own retention schedule, not ours.

We do not use your information to build profiles, and we do not send marketing blasts to people who simply asked for a price.

Who your information goes to

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information to third parties. Ever. We do not share it with data brokers, lead resellers, or other wrap shops.

The only parties that touch your data are the service providers we need to run a business: the messaging platforms you contacted us on (Meta and WhatsApp), our website host, and, if you become a customer, ordinary business services such as payment processing and accounting. Each handles data under its own agreement with us. We may also disclose information where the law requires it.

Photos of your vehicle

We photograph and film the work we do, and we post it on our social channels. If you would rather your vehicle not appear in our content, tell us before or during the job and we will keep it out. If something is already posted and you want it taken down, ask and we will remove it. License plates are not published.

Your choices and rights

  • Ask what we hold. You can ask what information we have about you and we will tell you.
  • Ask us to delete it. You can ask us to delete your enquiry and contact details, and we will, except where we are legally required to keep a record of completed work.
  • Correct it. If something we hold is wrong, tell us and we will fix it.
  • Stop hearing from us. Reply "stop" to any message from the studio, or just say so, and we will stop contacting you.
  • Opt out of ad tracking. You can limit the Meta Pixel through your Meta ad preferences, your browser's tracking protection, or a content blocker. The site works normally with the pixel blocked.

To exercise any of these, contact us using the details below. We do not charge for this and we will not treat you differently for asking.

Children

This site and our services are meant for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a child has sent us information, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

If we change how we handle information, we will update this page and the "last updated" date above. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect.
