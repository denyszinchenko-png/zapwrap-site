Why wrap, not paint

The smart money wraps the car.

It protects the paint you paid for.

Vinyl is a removable shield over your OEM finish. Years of sun, salt air and road grime hit the film, not the factory clear coat. Peel it later and the original paint is exactly as it left the line.

It's fully reversible.

Lease it, sell it, or change your mind in three years. A proper wrap comes off clean and returns the car to stock. A repaint is forever, and buyers can tell.

It lasts, and it costs less than paint.

Five to seven years with proper care, for a fraction of what a quality respray on an exotic runs. The look of a custom finish without touching the car's value.

Consult

Then

Install

Wrapped into the channels, not around them. Seams hidden, edges tucked, no lifting corners six months on.

Before you book

Questions owners actually ask.

How much does it cost to wrap a car?

It depends on vehicle size, body complexity, and film. Full color change wraps start at $3,000. Send photos and the exact year/make/model for an accurate quote.

Will a wrap damage my paint?

No. Properly installed and properly removed, vinyl protects your factory paint instead of damaging it - that's the whole premise of wrapping over painting.

How long does a wrap last?

5-7 years with proper care, depending on the film, installation quality, and how the vehicle is maintained.

Wrap or repaint: which is actually better?

For most owners, wrap wins: it costs less than a quality repaint, it's fully reversible, and it protects your factory paint instead of replacing it. Paint makes sense only if you want a permanent change.

Do you offer a warranty?

Yes - installation workmanship is covered. Ask us for the specific terms for your project; coverage depends on proper care and normal use versus damage.

What film brands do you use?

3M, Avery Dennison, and KPMF: professional-grade cast vinyl. We'll recommend the right one for your finish and budget.

Do you do PPF or window tint?

No. Vinyl wrap is our specialty and the only thing we do - that focus is why the install quality holds up.

Do you wrap boats?

Not yet - boat wraps are in development for the Naples marine community. Reach out and we'll notify you at launch.
